# AI Personality Matchmaker - V1 Diagnosis

## V1 Summary

Version 1 is a quiz to find your dating "type." It uses OpenRouter to analyze the user's short written answers and then gives them a personality type result. The quiz has 10 questions.

## Playtest Observations

Playing it felt a little awkward because the subject is dating, which is naturally a touchy and sometimes funny topic. Nothing felt especially confusing, but nothing felt especially fun either beyond that basic dating-quiz tension.

## AI Default Patterns Found

The project does not strongly feel "AI-made." The main detail that stood out as slightly default or artificial was the "Analyze My Match Style" button having a gradient and glow effect.

## V1 Diagnosis Sheet

### Question Quality

The questions are fine and fit the genre. They are not especially surprising, outrageous, or funny, and they mostly feel like what someone would expect from a standard dating-type quiz.

### Pacing

The pacing works well. Ten questions feels like an appropriate amount and can be finished in around 5 to 10 minutes.

### AI Analysis

The AI analysis is one of the stronger parts of the experience. It feels engaging because it gives a short summary about what the user likes and how they behave, rather than just listing traits.

### Results

The results are reasonably engaging, but the overall experience still feels standard. The end result works, but it does not yet feel especially memorable or surprising.

### Visuals

The design is very strong. The visuals feel nice and clean, and the orange palette and fonts are especially successful.

### First Impression

The first impression is positive because the app looks polished and appealing right away. The design helps it feel more finished than the core concept currently is.

### Replay Value

Replay value is low right now. It feels more like a one-time experience because the quiz is standard and not especially memorable yet.

### Shareability

Shareability is also limited in Version 1. It would be more likely to get shared if the questions or results were more outrageous, funny, or surprising.

## Target User

The quiz is for people who are curious about who they like. The user is imagined taking it casually on their phone, such as during a lunch break.

## Redesign Direction

Version 2 should feel more outrageous and funny. The goal is to make the questions and responses more ridiculous so the whole experience feels more entertaining and less like a standard dating quiz.

## Keep / Change / Delete List

### Keep

- The visual aesthetics
- The orange color palette
- The fonts
- The clean overall design feel
- The engaging AI summary style

### Change

- Change the quiz flow so only one question is visible at a time
- Add controls to move between questions instead of making the user scroll through the full page
- Make the experience more memorable and replayable
- Make the questions and results more outrageous and funny
- Shift the tone away from something standard and expected

### Delete

- Delete the dating-focused theme
- Delete the standard, predictable personality-quiz feel
- Delete the full-page stacked question layout that requires scrolling through every question
